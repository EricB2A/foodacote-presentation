Join the evolutionary Swiss mobile app to encourage food sharing

Foodacoté is a free food-sharing app that support environmentally-friendly people like you and I to share the surplus of food we have at home instead of throwing it away.

Mission

Builds social awareness and habits regarding food save & sustainability to motivate food waste reduction in private households. We are a mission oriented company who wants to change a cultural behavior!

Purpose

Strengthens local communities and encourage environmentally-conscious people to collaborate and work as a team to maximize the usage of food purchased in private homes. Connect with your neighbors and achieve ecological goals together!

Accessibility

Gives you access to food items closer to you using a geolocalization system & with a 24/7 online service. The platform is a C2C business model acting as a middleman. Come and meet your neighbors!

Footprint

Adds an emotional and monetary value to the food that would otherwise end up in the trash. Use this opportunity to give a second life to food items and the environment will thank you for it!

Food waste in Switzerland

One third of the food produced in Switzerland is lost or wasted along the food chain. This represents 300kg per person per year - resulting in 2’310’000’000kg. Together, we can make a difference!
